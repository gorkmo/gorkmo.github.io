## Tracks

Ambient Theme: Plays when the player is out of combat. Slow dungeon ambience with organ, choir, bells, etc.

Combat Theme: Switches from ambient theme when the player enters combat. Adds drums and increases the intensity of all instruments.

## Game Context

The assets are background music for a game level that are able to be swapped on any beat. The level takes place in a dungeon inhabited by skeletons and puzzles which serves as the tutorial level for the game.
